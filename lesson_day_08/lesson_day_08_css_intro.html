The Cottage Garden

The cottage garden is a distinct of gardenthat uses an informat design,dense plantings, and a mixture of ornamental and edible plants.
The Cottage Garden originated in England its history can be traced back for centuries, although they were re-invented in 1870's england ,when stylized were formed as a reaction to the more structured and rigorously mailtained English estate Gardens
The earliest cottage gardens were more practical than their modern descendants, with and emphasis on vegetables and herbs, along with some fruit trees.
